1.  How do I create a Spootr account?

Spootr uses your existing social network accounts to create an account. You can sign in to Spootr with your Facebook.

  2.  Who sees my contents when I post them to Spootr?

Your posts will be visible to all the members of the "Circle" that you have joined. Other users will be able to like your posts and put comments on it.

  3.  How do I report an inappropriate content when I see on Spootr?

On every post you can click on "three dots" at the top-right to report an inappropriate content.

  4.  How to I join a "Circle"?

Swipe to the right and go to "Circle". Find your campus and click "Plus" button.

  5.  How to I leave a "Circle"?

Swipe to the right and go to "Circle". Find your campus and click "Minus" button.

  6.  How do I delete one of my posts from Spootr?

Swipe to the right and go to "Profile". Scroll down and find the post that you want to delete. Click the "three dots" on the top-right of your post and then click "Delete".

  7.  How do I report a problem or suggestions?

Swipe to the right and go to "Settings". Click "Contact Us" button and send us an email. We will get back to you as soon as we can.

For additional inquiries, please e-mail us help@spootr.com
